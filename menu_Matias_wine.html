Matias Michelini

Matias is considered a forerunner of a movement of young winemakers with a different vision on how to make wine in Argentina, based on the idea of translating the landscape into wine with the least possible intervention. A cultivator of Biodynamics and a pioneer in the development of Gualtallary, today we find him as part of the Passionate Wines team."My style is that of an anti-hero, I don't follow any trend, since it doesn't last long. The wines I make show the character of the terroir where its grapes are born. A terroir where I chose to live and where to educate my children."
