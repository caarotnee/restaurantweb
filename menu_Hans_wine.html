Hans Vinding-Diers

Hans, a Danish national, was born in Stellenbosch, a South African wine-growing district. He grew up in Bordeaux, France, where his family owned the Chateau de Landiras in Graves. Over the years he has produced wines in Australia, Chile, France, Spain, Portugal, South Africa, Hungary, Argentina and Uruguay. In the late 1990s, working as a freelance with an English wine company, he was offered a joint-venture with an Argentine winery. In 1999 he made his first wine at the Humberto Canale winery. He fell in love with Patagonia and in the year 2000 he found the famous Mainqué vineyard planted in 1932, where Noemía is located today.
