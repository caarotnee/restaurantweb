Climates / Winkler Scales

This system was developed by Albert J. Winkler at the University of Davis, California in the mid-20th century. It is used to establish the characteristics of the wine-growing areas from a record of temperatures at the time of vine growth (from October 1 to April 30). From this system, 5 zones were established (from I to V) from the coldest to the warmest.
